Research Objectives

The ROBOMEDON project defines five key research objectives driving innovation in AI-powered medical diagnostics and robotic surgery.

O1: Cancer Diagnostics

Improvement of cancer diagnostics through automated processing and analysis of histopathological images using machine learning.

O2: Fusion Biopsy

Optimization of the fusion biopsy procedure for the prostate based on multiparametric MRI and ultrasound through machine learning methods.

O3: Robotic Surgery

Development of methods for precise intraoperative detection and preservation of neurovascular structures during robot-assisted radical prostatectomy.

O4: Video Analysis

Implementation of an intelligent system for the automatic analysis of intraoperative video in robotic surgery for real-time decision support.

O5: Clinical Integration

Advancement of AI integration into clinical practice through interpretable models for medical data analysis adapted to diagnostic and surgical protocols.
