About the Project

ROBOMEDON is a five-year research project focused on the development and implementation of artificial intelligence methods for medical diagnostics and robotic surgery in oncology. The project addresses the urgent need for early diagnosis of prostate cancer, breast cancer, and dermatological disorders — diseases where early detection significantly increases the chances of successful treatment and improves patients' quality of life.

The project integrates interdisciplinary collaboration between experts in computer science, medicine, biotechnology, and healthcare informatics, fostering the development of robust, transparent, and generalizable AI models through the integration of multimodal data — genomic, histological, and clinical.
