Work Packages

The project consists of five work packages, each corresponding to a specific thematic group outlined in the project objectives.

WP1: Histopathological Image Analysis

Development of methods for processing and analysis of histopathological images. Focuses on integrating whole-slide imaging (WSI) with machine learning for detection and classification of pathological changes in breast, prostate, and dermatological tissue samples using genomic, histological, and clinical data.

WP2: Precision Fusion Biopsy

Development of advanced machine learning methods for precise fusion biopsy of the prostate based on multiparametric MRI and ultrasound. Aims to enhance biopsy precision, reduce reliance on physicians' cognitive abilities, and enable a standardized, objective approach to prostate biopsy.

WP3: Neurovascular Detection in Robotic Prostatectomy

Development of an AI-assisted system for intraoperative detection and preservation of neurovascular structures during Da Vinci robot-assisted radical prostatectomy using hyperspectral analysis, enabling real-time visualization of key anatomical structures.

WP4: Intraoperative Video Analysis

Development of an intelligent system for analyzing intraoperative videos in robotic surgery, focusing on automatic detection of surgical instruments, identification of anatomical structures, and recognition of key surgical phases to provide real-time recommendations and alerts.

WP5: Clinical AI Integration

Advancing the integration of artificial intelligence into clinical practice by developing scalable, modular, and reliable AI systems. Focuses on model interpretability, explainable AI (XAI), regulatory compliance with the AI Act and GDPR, and transparent implementation in hospital systems.
